Welcome to Belfast Pilates & Physiotherapy

Specialists in Physio led Pilates in Belfast

Belfast Pilates & Physiotherapy is well known throughout Northern Ireland as spinal and sports injury rehabilitation physios with close links to both GP and Consultants.

We are all Chartered Physiotherapists and APPI Pilates Instructors who assess and treat back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, musculoskeletal conditions, joint problems, arthritic pain, headaches, postural aches and pain. We teach Mat-work and Reformer Pilates both in group classes and 1:1 sessions.
